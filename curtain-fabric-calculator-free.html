Why Use a Free Curtain Fabric Calculator?

Buying fabric for curtains is one of the easiest places to overspend — or underspend and run out mid-project. A free curtain fabric calculator eliminates both risks. Instead of guessing "how many yards for curtains" and ending up with leftover fabric you cannot return, you get an exact number based on your actual window dimensions, chosen fullness, hem allowances, and fabric width.

Most fabric stores do not accept returns on cut fabric. A miscalculation that is off by even half a yard can cost you $10-30 on decor fabrics. For a full set of living room curtains, the margin of error compounds quickly. Using a free curtain fabric calculator before you shop means you walk into the store with a precise number — no second trips, no wasted material.

Pro tip: Print the result or email it to yourself. Most fabric stores will help you double-check your yardage at the cutting counter, but they will not take returns on cut fabric if your number is wrong.

Free Curtain Fabric Calculator vs. Store Calculators

Many fabric store websites offer a "free curtain fabric calculator," but there are important differences in what they calculate. Store calculators often skip the fullness ratio or use a generic default — which means you end up buying less fabric than you actually need for a professional look.

This free curtain fabric calculator lets you adjust every variable: fullness ratio (1.5x to 3x), hem allowance, header allowance, pattern repeat, fabric width, and waste percentage. It also toggles between inches and centimeters. No account. No email. No limits.

Yes. Calculate yardage once for the face fabric, then buy the same amount for lining fabric. For interlined drapes (face + interlining + lining), multiply by three.
