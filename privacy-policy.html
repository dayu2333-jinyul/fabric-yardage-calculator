Our Privacy Commitment

YardageCalculator is built with a straightforward approach to privacy: all fabric yardage calculations, fabric width conversions, and project estimators run entirely in your browser using client-side JavaScript. No data you enter into any of our calculators is ever sent to, stored on, or processed by any server. We designed the site this way intentionally — the numbers you input for your sewing projects stay on your device.

Information We Collect

YardageCalculator.com does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Because the site is fully static and serverless, there is no backend database, no user accounts, no comment system, and no form submission that captures personal data. All calculations are performed locally in your browser. No data you enter into our fabric yardage calculator, width converter, fabric converter, pillow fabric calculator, or quilt fabric calculator is sent to our servers.

Cookies

YardageCalculator uses no cookies for any purpose. We do not use Google Analytics, advertising cookies, tracking cookies, third-party analytics services, or any form of user tracking. The site is fully static and served from Cloudflare's edge network. Cloudflare may set its own strictly necessary cookies for performance and security (e.g., __cf_bm for bot management), but these do not track your activity across sites and are not used for advertising or analytics.

Local Storage

Some tools on this site may use your browser's localStorage to temporarily store fabric measurements you entered so they persist if you accidentally refresh the page. This data stays exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to any server. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's local storage.

Third-Party Services

We do not use Google Analytics, advertising networks, affiliate trackers, social media pixels, data brokers, or any third-party services that collect user data. The site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages, and Cloudflare's edge network processes requests for the sole purpose of delivering web pages. No user data is logged, stored, or shared by us. For Cloudflare's data processing practices, see Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

Since we do not collect any user data, there is nothing to retain. If your browser stores any calculation results in localStorage, that data persists until you clear it. We have no access to this data and cannot retrieve or delete it — it is entirely under your control.

Your Rights

Because we collect no personal data, there is little to exercise rights over. If you have concerns about data processing by Cloudflare's edge network (whose infrastructure serves this site), please refer to Cloudflare's privacy policy. For any other questions, contact us at the domain email address.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or to comply with legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
